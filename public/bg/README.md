# Background Images

Place your background images for each slide here:

- `slide1.png` - Background for slide 1
- `slide2.png` - Background for slide 2
- `slide3.png` - Background for slide 3
- `slide4.png` - Background for slide 4
- `slide5.png` - Background for slide 5

## Image Specifications

- **Format**: PNG (recommended) or JPG
- **Size**: Recommended 1920x1080 or larger (images will be scaled and cropped)
- **Style**: Abstract floating drawings that match your design aesthetic
- **Optimization**: Compress images for web (aim for < 500KB each)

The images will be displayed at 180% width/height and centered, with parallax effects on desktop.
